Project Overview

The Latitude project was an opportunity to experiment with APIs and visualizations. The premise is nice and easy - 'how does that latitude of a city impact the weather in that city?'. Sure, you and I will both assume that cities in whichever hemisphere is currently experiencing summer, as well as those closest to the equator are going to be warmest (spoiler: you're right), but can we prove it? And not just with historic data, but real time data?! Sounds like an API adventure!
